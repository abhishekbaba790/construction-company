About us

PROSAVA PRIVATE LIMITED company provides design, engineering, procurement, construction and integrated project management services from ‘Concept to Commissioning’ with highest quality and safety standards.

Explore Our Services

We provide solutions within our four core offerings:

Project conception and engineering development

We deliver :

  1. Conceptual plan and layout of facilities.
  2. Preliminary economic assessment .
  3. Pre-feasibility study .
  4. Basic engineering, process optimization and flow sheets.
  5. Detailed engineering, civil and structural details, piping and electrical layout, instrumentation and control engineering.
  6. Detailed cost estimation.

Failure analysis, loss assessment & Rehabilitation projects

We have experts & well experienced workment to undertake following tasks :

  1. Analysis of failure and assessment of loss.
  2. Study of deterioration of structures .
  3. Develop & recommend repair methodology .
  4. Prepair repair estimates .
  5. Undertake repair & rehabilitation jobs.

Manufacturing & Spare Parts development

Our well equipped workshop at Faridabad is capable to develop & deliver :

  1. Material handling spares like Air slides, screw conveyors, conveyor spares.
  2. Spares for process piping, ash handling etc.
  3. Customized equipment for domestic & Industrial waste management.

Construction monitoring and quality assurance

We excel in :

  1. Planning organizing and scheduling: Listing of all major tasks, preparation of WBS, estimation of time and cost, Assessment of dependency and development of critical path.
  2. Ensuring effective manpower and equipment mobilization.
  3. Ensuring performance of construction contract.
  4. Construction Management and supervision.
  5. Cost control and quality checks.
  6. Commisioning support.
  7. Startup assistance.
  8. Post implementation review.
